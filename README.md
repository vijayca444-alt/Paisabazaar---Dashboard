📊 Project Summary

This project presents an interactive Banking Customer Analysis Dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI to analyze customer financial behavior, credit profiles, and key banking performance indicators. The dashboard transforms raw banking data into meaningful business insights by examining customer income, outstanding debt, monthly balances, credit utilization, and credit history.
Through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and dynamic filtering, the dashboard helps identify patterns in customer financial health and credit score distribution. It enables stakeholders to compare customer segments, monitor financial trends, and make informed, data-driven decisions.
The project demonstrates how Business Intelligence tools can convert large volumes of banking data into clear, actionable insights that support customer analysis, financial planning, and strategic decision-making.

⭐ Key Highlights

Built an interactive Power BI dashboard for banking customer analysis.
Analyzed financial indicators including income, debt, monthly balance, and credit utilization.
Compared customer performance across different credit score categories.
Designed dynamic KPI cards and interactive visualizations using DAX.
Implemented Power Query for data cleaning and transformation.
Created slicers and filters for interactive data exploration.
Delivered business insights through effective dashboard design and storytelling.

🎯 Business Impact

The dashboard provides financial institutions with a comprehensive view of customer financial health, enabling them to better understand customer segments, monitor credit-related metrics, identify financial trends, and support data-driven business decisions. These insights can improve customer profiling, strengthen financial planning, and enhance strategic decision-making.
